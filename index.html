Detailed Itinerary

  • Intercontinental flight from Amsterdam (AMS) to São Paulo (GRU)
  • **Accommodation:** Pullman São Paulo Guarulhos Airport (1 night, L&B)

Day 2: Thursday, July 24 – São Paulo to Bonito

  • Local flight from São Paulo (GRU) to Campo Grande (CGR).
  • Drive from Campo Grande to Bonito (approx. 4 hours).
  • **Accommodation:** Pousada Olho d'Água.

Day 3: Friday, July 25 – Bonito Activities

  • Snorkeling in the Rio da Prata.
  • Visit Buraco das Araras, a 124m deep, 500m wide crater. A lake with caimans is at the bottom.

Day 4: Saturday, July 26 – Bonito Activity

  • Snorkeling Lagoa Misteriosa (Mysterious Lake). This sinkhole lake is one of Brazil's deepest underwater caves.

Day 5: Sunday, July 27 – Bonito to Pantanal

  • Drive from Bonito to the Pantanal (approx. 4 hours).
  • **Accommodation:** Pousada Aguapé.

Day 6-8: Monday, July 28 – Wednesday, July 30 – Pantanal Exploration

  • Explore the Pantanal.
  • Horseback riding, hiking, photo safaris, and boat trips.

Day 8: Wednesday, July 30 – Pantanal to Campo Grande

  • Drive from Pantanal accommodation to Campo Grande (approx. 3.5 hours).
  • **Accommodation:** Hotel Vale Verde.

Day 9: Thursday, July 31 – Campo Grande to Foz do Iguaçu

  • Local flights: Campo Grande (CGR) to Campinas (VCP), then Campinas (VCP) to Foz do Iguaçu (IGU).
  • **Accommodation:** Vivaz Cataratas Hotel Resort.

Day 10: Friday, August 1 – Foz do Iguaçu (Brazilian Side)

  • Half-day visit to the Brazilian side of the Iguaçu Falls.
  • Macuco safari boat trip: a spectacular journey to the bottom of the falls by speedboat, preceded by an open jeep ride through the national park.

Day 11: Saturday, August 2 – Foz do Iguaçu (Argentinian Side)

  • Full-day visit to the Argentinian side of the Iguaçu Falls. Experience the "Garganta del Diablo" (Devil's Throat) up close.

Day 12: Sunday, August 3 – Foz do Iguaçu to Rio de Janeiro

  • Local flight from Foz do Iguaçu (IGU) to Rio de Janeiro (GIG).
  • **Accommodation:** Bossa Nova Ipanema Hotel.
  • Rio de Janeiro, the 'Cidade Maravilhosa,' offers iconic sights like Sugarloaf Mountain and Christ the Redeemer, and vibrant beaches.

Day 13: Monday, August 4 – Rio de Janeiro Landmarks

  • Full-day excursion to Corcovado (Christ the Redeemer statue) and Sugarloaf Mountain.

Day 14: Tuesday, August 5 – Rio de Janeiro Culture & Nightlife

  • Half-day bike tour cycling from Copacabana along Guanabara Bay towards Lapa and the historic center, passing iconic neighborhoods, cemeteries, and the famous Selarón Steps.
  • Carioca nightlife tour exploring less touristy spots with live music (Samba or Forró).

Day 15: Wednesday, August 6 – Free Day in Rio

  • Free day to explore.

Day 16: Thursday, August 7 – Departure from Rio

  • Intercontinental flight from Rio de Janeiro (GIG) to Amsterdam (AMS).

Day 17: Friday, August 8 – Arrival in Amsterdam

  • Arrival in Amsterdam.
